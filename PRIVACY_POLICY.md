# Privacy Policy - Sista Events & Rentals

**Last Updated:** February 7, 2026

## Introduction

Sista Events & Rentals ("we," "our," or "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you visit our website or use our services.

## Information We Collect

### Personal Information
When you make a booking or contact us, we may collect:
- **Name** - To identify you and personalize our service
- **Email Address** - To send booking confirmations and communicate with you
- **Phone Number** - To contact you regarding your booking
- **Event Details** - Date, type of event, and requirements
- **Message Content** - Any additional information you provide

### Automatically Collected Information
- **IP Address** - For security and analytics
- **Browser Type and Version** - To optimize website performance
- **Device Information** - To ensure mobile compatibility
- **Usage Data** - Pages visited, time spent, and interactions

## How We Use Your Information

We use your personal information to:
1. **Process Bookings** - Confirm and manage your event rental requests
2. **Communication** - Respond to inquiries and send booking updates
3. **Service Improvement** - Analyze usage patterns to enhance user experience
4. **Security** - Protect against fraud and unauthorized access
5. **Legal Compliance** - Fulfill our legal obligations

## Data Storage and Security

### Security Measures
- **Encryption** - All data transmitted via HTTPS/TLS
- **Firebase Security** - Industry-standard Google Cloud infrastructure
- **Access Controls** - Limited employee access to personal data
- **Rate Limiting** - Protection against brute force attacks
- **Input Validation** - Sanitization of all user inputs

### Data Retention
- **Booking Data** - Retained for 3 years for business records
- **Contact Inquiries** - Retained for 1 year
- **Analytics Data** - Anonymized and retained for 2 years

## Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:
- **Firebase (Google)** - Authentication and database hosting
- **Twilio** - SMS notifications (optional)
- These services have their own privacy policies
